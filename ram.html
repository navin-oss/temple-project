Shri Ram Mandir

The Eternal Home of Maryada Purushottam

Sacred Timeline

Ancient Legacy

Mentioned in ancient scriptures including Valmiki Ramayana (6th-5th BCE) and Skanda Purana. The original temple stood here since 12th century CE

Architectural Marvel

Built in Nagara style with 360 carved pillars, 3-story structure covering 2.77 acres. Made without iron using traditional stone interlocking

Divine Sanctum

Houses the childhood form of Ram Lalla (5-year-old Shri Ram) carved from 2.5 billion-year-old black sandstone
