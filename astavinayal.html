Ashtavinayak Ganpati

Discover the divine grace of Lord Ganesha at the eight sacred Ashtavinayak temples.

About Ashtavinayak

Ashtavinayak refers to the eight ancient temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha in Maharashtra. Each temple is steeped in history and mythology, offering devotees a unique glimpse into the divine manifestations of Ganpati. Pilgrims visit these temples to seek blessings, wisdom, and success, making the journey a spiritual and cultural celebration.

The Eight Sacred Temples

Moreshwar Temple

Location: Morgaon, Pune

Significance: The most important of the AshtaVinayak temples, representing the beginning of the pilgrimage.

Siddhivinayak Temple

Location: Siddhatek, Ahmednagar

Significance: Known for its powerful deity, believed to fulfill wishes. The only Ashtavinayak with the trunk on the right.

Ballaleshwar Temple

Location: Pali, Raigad

Significance: The only Ganesha named after a devotee, representing devotion.

Varadavinayak Temple

Location: Mahad, Raigad

Significance: Known for granting boons to devotees.

Chintamani Temple

Location: Theur, Pune

Significance: Believed to dispel worries and grant peace of mind.

Girijatmaj Temple

Location: Lenyadri, Pune

Significance: Located in a cave complex, representing Ganesha as a child of Parvati.

Vigneshwar Temple

Location: Ozar, Pune

Significance: Believed to remove obstacles and grant success.

Mahavinayak Temple

Location: Ranjangaon, Pune

Significance: A powerful and majestic form of Ganesha.
