Dagdusheth Ganpati: The Divine Icon

A beacon of faith, devotion, and grandeur in Pune.

A Timeless Legacy

A Rich Heritage

The temple, established in the late 19th century by Dagdusheth Halwai and his wife Lakshmibai, remains one of the most revered shrines in Maharashtra.

Ganeshotsav Grand Celebrations

The temple's annual Ganeshotsav attracts millions of devotees, witnessing grand aartis, bhajans, and cultural festivities.

A Pillar of Service

The temple trust actively engages in social welfare, supporting education, healthcare, and community initiatives.

Dagdusheth Ganpati is not just a temple; it is a spiritual and cultural icon of Maharashtra.
